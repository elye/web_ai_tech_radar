## Overview
Nano Banana is Google DeepMind's latest image editing model, integrated into the Gemini app. It excels at maintaining character likeness, blending multiple photos, and enabling advanced multi-turn editing for creative and personal use.

## Key Benefits
- Top-rated global image editing quality
- Consistent identity preservation across edits
- Multi-photo blending and composition
- Multi-turn, iterative editing workflows
- Style transfer and creative design features
